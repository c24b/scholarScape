Get Started !

scholarScape is designed to make your life easy when dealing with Google Scholar. It enables you to launch crawling campaigns on a particular word, a particular author or a particular URL. scholarScape will then :

  • store all the publications on this page
  • click on all the cited by "links" and fetch all the publications on those pages too
  • and again until a certain depth has been reached
An image is worth a thousand word :

You can then export a graph of the relations between publications. Publications are linked when one cites the other.

Organisation

The are two main pages in scholarScape : launch and explore.

The launch section is where you launch your crawling campaigns. You only have to fill in the project your campaign will belong to, your campaign name, the start titles/authors/urls, the depth of the crawl.

The explore section is where you monitor your campaign, you can see the latest items that have been retrieved and some infos about the campaign.
